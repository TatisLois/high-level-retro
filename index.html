Blameless Retrospectives

Outcome

Evaluate our past sprint cycle and gather feedback on our wins and areas of opportunities.

Philosophy

Go hard on process, soft on people.

Encourage honesty and impartiality in devising and assessing solutions to problems

It’s all about the follow-up.

Structure

    Format

    • Rotating facilitator
    • Full team participation
    • 30 minute Visible Timer (to start)
    • A set of four questions (Went well, can improve, action items, further clarity)

Our outcome does not change.

Our structure evolves with the team.

The Prime Directive

"Regardless of what we discover, we understand and truly believe that everyone did the best job they could, given what they knew at the time, their skills and abilities, the resources available, and the situation at hand."

Food for thought

Contributing causes. Identify contributing causes, rather than trying to identify a single root cause. Modern software systems are complex

Silly but effective are the “Five Whys”. Whenever we’re looking at an outcome or a bug, ask why 5 times.

Maintain a neutral environment by caring about how you frame our problems
